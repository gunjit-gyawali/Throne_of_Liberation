Fifteen years ago, the sky shattered. Fire rained down, and from it came beings that looked human—but weren’t. The six great lands, once ruled by powerful Archons, fell one by one... until silence claimed them all. Now, the world is broken, divided, and afraid. In the quiet Land of the Present, Kiyoshi Aetherius discovers a ring buried deep in the forest—a relic that shouldn’t exist, pulsing with a power that shouldn’t be here. The moment he wears it, something ancient awakens. Whispers of the dead. A name long forgotten. A seal beginning to break. Chosen by a legacy buried beneath blood and silence, Kiyoshi is about to uncover a truth the world was never meant to remember. Some say the Archons were heroes. Some say they were gods. Kiyoshi will become something far more dangerous.
